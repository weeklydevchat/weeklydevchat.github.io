You've probabably heard of this technique.  You have a problem you are stuck on, and you "explain" it to some kind of object (e.g. rubber duck).  The idea is the act of explaining it helps you spot what you've missed.  

Do you do this?  What other tricks work for you?  Examples... 
  - Walking away and taking a break
  - Changing to another task
  - Sleeping on it
  - Asking the "five whys"
  - Whiteboarding / visualising
